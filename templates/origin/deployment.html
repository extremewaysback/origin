Few lines of code to deploy your project on Heroku:

  1. $git init (init a git repository)
  2. $git add .
  3. $git commit -m "NAME" (actually record the snapshot with a NMAE)
  4. $heroku login (log into your heroku count)
  5. $git push https://git.heroku.com/YOURAPPNAMEonHeroku.git master
